Millionaires Shortbread

Ingredients

For the shortbread

  • 225g softened unsalted butter
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 340g plain flour

For the caramel

  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 75g golden syrup
  • 120g butter

For the Topping

  • 300g milk chocolate

Method

  1. Cream together butter and sugar
  2. Add the flour and salt
  3. Tip the mixture out onto a clean surface
  4. Knead it to form a smooth dough
  5. Put the dough into an 8x8 lined square tin and even it out
  6. Bake at 150C for 35 mins, or until slightly golden on top
  7. For the caramel, add the sugar and golden syrup together in a saucepan
  8. Then add the unsalted butter and condensed milk
  9. Melt the butter and then heat on a very low, steady boil - this takes about 20 mins and you must whisk it constantly or it will burn!
  10. Pour onto the shortbread and smooth it out
  11. Let this cool down completely
  12. Melt 200g of the chocolate
  13. Tip into a cool bowl and add the rest of the chocolate. Stir until melted
  14. Pour on top of the shortbread
  15. Let this set completely before you cut it into slices
