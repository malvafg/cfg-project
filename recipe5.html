Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 345g plain flour
  • 60g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 7g instant yeast
  • 120ml water
  • 60ml milk
  • 45g butter
  • 1 egg

For the filling

  • 70g butter, softened
  • 1.5 tbsp cinnamon
  • 60g dark soft brown sugar

For the glaze

  • 120g icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Method

  1. Whisk together the flour, caster sugar, salt and yeast
  2. Mix the water, milk and butter together and microwave for a minute
  3. Add this to the dry ingredients
  4. Add an egg and whisk until the dough starts coming away from the side of the bowl
  5. Dust surface with flour and knead for 5 minutes
  6. Put in a lightly greased bowl for 15 minutes covered with a tea towel
  7. For the filling, mix together the butter, cinnamon and brown sugar
  8. Dust surface with flour again and roll into a rectangle 17 inches by about 12 inches
  9. Spread the filling on top
  10. Roll it gently and trim the edges
  11. Chop the roll into 10 buns
  12. Lay out in a tin, spaced out
  13. Put tinfoil over this and leave somewhere warm for 1.5 hours
  14. Bake at 170C for 25 minutes
  15. For the glaze, whisk the icing sugar, milk, salt and vanilla extract together
  16. Pour the glaze onto the cinnamon buns as soon as they come out of the oven
