Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 84g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar

Method

  1. Melt the butter and dark chocolate together
  2. Leave the melted mixture to cool to room temperature
  3. Preheat oven to 160C fan while you're waiting for the mixture to cool
  4. Line 20cm square tin with baking paper
  5. Chop the white chocolate and milk chocolate into chunks
  6. Break the eggs into a bowl and add the sugar. Whisk this with an electric mixer until thick and creamy
  7. Pour the melted chocolate into the egg mixture and fold with a spatula
  8. Sieve the flour and cocoa powder into the bowl and then fold this in
  9. Stir in the chocolate chunks
  10. Pour into the tin and put it in the oven for 25 mins or until the top has a shiny, papery crust
